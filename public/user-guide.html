Managing Volunteers

Go to the Volunteers tab to add new members to the ministry.

1
Click Volunteer Summary (or navigate to /volunteers) to view the list.
2
Use the "Add New Volunteer" form (usually found in the Availability page or dedicated Manage section) to onboard new members.

Managing Availability

The Availability page is where you configure who can serve when. Click on any volunteer's name to open their settings:

Volunteer Level

Toggle between Lector (Readers) and Volunteer (Experienced/All Roles).

Time Preferences

Set "Always Available", "Available ONLY at...", or "Available EXCEPT..." specific times.

Specific Dates

Click dates on the calendar to mark them as Unavailable (Red). You can also set a date range.

Indefinite Leave

Check "Mark as Unavailable Indefinitely" for members on long-term leave.
If you mark a volunteer as unavailable on a date they are already assigned, the system will warn you and offer to auto-repair the schedule!

Generating the Schedule

Go to the Schedule tab to create or view the monthly rostrum.

1
Select the Month and Year.
2
Click Generate Schedule. The system will automatically assign volunteers based on history and fairness rules.
3
Review: Use the table to verify assignments.

Special Masses

Need to add a wedding, funeral, or holiday mass?

  • Click "+ Add Special Mass" in the Schedule tab.
  • Enter the Date, Time, Name, and Description.
  • The system will suggest assignments, or you can leave them to be filled later.

‚ùì FAQ

Q: How do I print the schedule?

A: Go to the Summary page and click the "Download PDF Summary" button for a clean, printable version.

Q: Can I edit assignments manually?

A: You can edit mass details (time/name) using the Edit (pencil) button. To change extended assignments, it is best to update the volunteer's availability for that day and click Regenerate to let the system find the best replacement.

Q: What does the "Attire" section mean?

A: Symbols like üëï (Casual) and üëî (Gala) indicate the required dress code for the mass type (Weekday vs Sunday/Special).
