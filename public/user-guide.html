üë• Managing Volunteers

Adding Volunteers

1 Go to the "Volunteers" tab in the navigation menu
2 Click "Add New Volunteer"
3 Enter the volunteer's name and click "Add"
Add all your volunteers before generating the schedule for best results.

Managing Volunteer Availability

1 Go to the "Update Availability" tab
2 Click on any volunteer's name to open their calendar
3 Click on dates when they are unavailable (dates turn red)
4 Click "Save Changes" when done

Marking a Volunteer as Unavailable Indefinitely

1 Open the volunteer's calendar (click their name)
2 Check the box: "Mark as Unavailable Indefinitely"
3 This prevents them from being assigned to future schedules
Use this for volunteers on extended leave or who have temporarily stopped serving.

üìÜ Creating & Managing Schedules

Generating a New Monthly Schedule

1 Go to the "Schedule" tab
2 Select the Month and Year from the dropdowns
3 Click "Generate Schedule"
4 The app will automatically assign volunteers to all masses
The schedule considers volunteer availability and ensures fair distribution of assignments.

Adding Special Masses

For Christmas, Easter, or other special occasions:

1 In the Schedule tab, click "+ Add Special Mass"
2 Select the Date and Time
3 Enter a name (e.g., "Christmas Eve Mass")
4 Manually assign volunteers to each role
5 Click "Add Mass"

Regenerating the Schedule

If you want to create new assignments with different volunteers:

1 Click the "Regenerate" button
2 The app will create new assignments while keeping Special Masses
Regenerate if you're not happy with the initial assignments or if volunteer availability changes.

üìã Understanding the Schedule

Schedule Table Columns

  • Date: The day of the mass
  • Time: Mass time (e.g., 6:00 AM, 7:30 AM)
  • Type: Weekday, Sunday, or Special
  • Lector1: First reader
  • Commentator: Commentator for the mass
  • Lector2: Second reader (Sunday masses only)

Mass Types & Attire

Weekday / Casual: Mon-Fri masses & Sat morning.
Ladies: Black Pants & White Polo Shirt
Men: Black Pants & White Polo Shirt
Sunday / Gala: All Sunday masses & Sat evening.
Ladies: Black Skirt & White Blouse
Men: Black Pants & White Polo Shirt

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I edit individual assignments?

A: Currently, you need to regenerate the schedule or add a Special Mass with manual assignments. Future updates may include direct editing.

Q: What if a volunteer becomes unavailable after the schedule is created?

A: Mark them as unavailable in the "Update Availability" tab, then click "Regenerate" to create a new schedule without them.

Q: How does the app ensure fairness?

A: The algorithm:

  • Distributes assignments evenly across all volunteers
  • Avoids assigning the same person on consecutive days
  • Limits weekday assignments to 1 per week per volunteer
  • Rotates roles and partners for variety

Q: Can multiple people use the app at the same time?

A: Yes! Changes are saved to the cloud and sync automatically.

Q: How do I print the schedule?

A: Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) while viewing the Schedule tab.
